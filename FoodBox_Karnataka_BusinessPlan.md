## 4. Market Research (Lesson 13)

**Competitor Analysis:**
- Box8, Swiggy Daily, local dabbawallas
- Gap identified: No subscription service targeting IT workers with diet-specific options

**Survey Simulation (60 target users):**
- 78% unhappy with current lunch options
- 64% willing to pay ₹100–₹200/day for consistent home-style food
- Top complaints: inconsistent quality, no customisation, poor hygiene

## 5. Revenue Model (Lesson 15)

| Plan | Price | Orders/day |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Basic (1 meal/day) | ₹3,500/month | 1 tiffin |
| Premium (2 meals/day) | ₹6,000/month | 2 tiffins |
| Weekend Special | ₹800/month add-on | Sat–Sun only |

**Break-even Analysis:**
- Fixed costs/month: ₹65,000 (kitchen + staff + rent)
- Variable cost per tiffin: ₹55
- Selling price per tiffin: ₹115
- Contribution margin per tiffin: ₹60
- **Break-even: 1,084 tiffins/month = ~180 subscribers**

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):**
- Average subscription: 8 months
- Average monthly spend: ₹3,500
- CLV = ₹28,000 per customer

## 6. Store Expansion Plan (Lesson 18 + Lesson 14)

### Phase 1 — Online Only (Months 1–5)
- D2C delivery via Swiggy Instamart partner + own WhatsApp orders
- Target: 500 subscribers

### Phase 2 — First Kiosk (Month 6)
**Location:** Whitefield (IT hub, 80,000+ daily footfall)

| Kiosk Cost | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| One-time setup | ₹45,000 |
| Monthly rent | ₹8,000 |
| Staff | ₹12,000 |
| Supplies | ₹5,000 |
| Utilities | ₹2,000 |
| **Total monthly** | **₹27,000** |

**Break-even for kiosk:** 95 additional orders/month at ₹285 avg contribution

### Phase 3 — 3 Kiosks (Month 12)
- Whitefield → Koramangala → HSR Layout
- Projected daily orders: 1,200 across all locations
- Projected monthly revenue: ₹13.8 lakhs

### Supply Chain Considerations (Lesson 14)
- Central kitchen model: 1 kitchen serves all 3 kiosks
- Cold chain logistics: 3-hour delivery windows
- Vendor management: 2 verified vegetable suppliers, 1 dairy partner
- IoT temperature monitoring for delivery containers
