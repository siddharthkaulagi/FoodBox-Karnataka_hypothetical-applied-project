## Strategy Summary

### Target Audience
Working professionals, 25–35, Bengaluru, ₹40,000+ monthly income, health-conscious, no time to cook.

### Channel Mix
| Channel | Budget | Purpose |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Instagram Ads | ₹12,000 | Awareness + brand building |
| Google Search Ads | ₹10,000 | High-intent capture |
| WhatsApp Business | ₹3,000 | Retention + re-engagement |
| Content Creation | ₹5,000 | Reels, testimonials, SEO blogs |
| **Total** | **₹30,000** | |

### SMART Goal
- **Specific:** 500 paid subscribers
- **Measurable:** CPL ≤ ₹60, ROAS ≥ 3×
- **Achievable:** Based on Bengaluru market size of 1.2M working professionals
- **Realistic:** Budget allocated per industry benchmarks for food delivery niche
- **Timely:** 90 days from launch

## Store Expansion Model (Phase 2+)

After reaching 500 online subscribers, the next growth lever is physical pickup kiosks.

### Kiosk Cost Structure
| Item | Monthly Cost |
|------|-------------|
| Rent (small kiosk, 80 sq ft) | ₹8,000 |
| Staff (1 person) | ₹12,000 |
| Packaging & supplies | ₹5,000 |
| Misc / utilities | ₹2,000 |
| **Total monthly opex** | **₹27,000** |

**One-time setup cost:** ₹45,000 (equipment, branding, deposit)

**Break-even:** 95 additional orders/month at ₹285 average order contribution margin

### Expansion Locations (Phase 3)
- Whitefield (IT corridor, high density)
- Koramangala (startup hub)
- HSR Layout (young working population)
